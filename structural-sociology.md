### CC: Coupling Completeness
Social cohesion = coupling density. Inequality = coupling asymmetry. Gini coefficient measures coupling entropy. Segregation = coupling partition — structural violence.

### NLI: Node-Link Identity
Individual and society are not separate. You ARE your social relations. No 'individual' apart from coupling network.

### MSC: Meta-Structural Convergence
Societies converge toward attractors. Revolution = structural phase transition. Social collapse = decoherence cascade.
